# Code of Conduct

- When contributing, we expect you to respect the project's core technical principles.
  A primary focus is environmental awareness.
  The goal is to minimize resource consumption (especially storage and memory)
  and maximize the efficiency of on-the-fly computation over pre-generation and materialization.
- This is an open community, participation is not restricted by personal background or viewpoints.
  Contributions are evaluated solely based on their alignment with the project's technical interests.
- Ideologies are natural social phenomena, but any irrelevant ideological remarks are discouraged.
  Participants are independent agents responsible for their own communication style and its social consequences.
  Maintainers do not act as moral arbiters in interpersonal friction.
  However, extreme or abusive behavior, obstruction of the project's progress,
  or violations of legal requirements may result in intervention.
- You are personally responsible for all content you submit.
  By contributing, you implicitly declare that your submission is legal, not plagiarized,
  free of malicious code, and eligible for release under the project's license.
- You acknowledge that the content you submit will become part of the project under its permissive license,
  and may be subject to further modifications and refactoring by others.
